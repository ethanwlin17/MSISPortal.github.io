Congratulations on your admission to the Kelley MSIS Program

You've joined a dynamic community where technology meets business, and ambition meets opportunity. As an MSIS student at the Kelley School of Business, you're stepping into a world of innovation, collaboration, and real-world impact. Here, you'll develop the skills to lead in a digital economy, solve complex challenges, and shape the future of business through technology. This is more than a program—it's a launchpad for your next chapter. Welcome to the journey.

Your next steps:

We know that starting your MSIS journey comes with a mix of excitement and questions. That's why we've designed this resource hub to support you through every step ahead. Whether you're navigating course registration, exploring housing options, or preparing for life in Bloomington, this page is your go-to guide to help you transition with clarity and confidence.
